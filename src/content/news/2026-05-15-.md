Nunawading Kangas Basketball Club is excited to announce a special Coach the Coaches evening in partnership with ShootHQ, the Notting Hill-based training facility backed by some of the finest credentials in Australian basketball.

The session, scheduled for Sunday evening in late June, will be led by ShootHQ's Joey Wright - a three-time NBL Coach of the Year - alongside co-founder Adam Gibson, who spent 16 years in the NBL, won championship titles, and represented the Boomers at the 2012 London Olympics and multiple FIBA World Championships. It's a rare opportunity for our coaching community to learn directly from two people who have operated at the very highest levels of the game.

With 94 junior teams across the club this season, Nunawading Kangas has one of the largest and most dedicated volunteer coaching groups in the region. Events like this are a reflection of the club's ongoing commitment to supporting those coaches - because when our coaches grow, our players grow with them.

We're expecting strong interest from across the club and spaces are expected to fill quickly, so stay tuned for registration details coming soon. Whether you're coaching your first season or your fifteenth, this is an evening well worth your time.

A big thank you to Joey, Adam and the team at ShootHQ for making this happen, and to Terence Leong from our Coaching Academy for helping bring it all together.
