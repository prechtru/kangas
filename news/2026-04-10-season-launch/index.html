Season Launch Night Brings the Club Together

Players, families, and coaches packed the stadium for a high-energy launch night ahead of the winter season.

By Club Committee 10 April 2026

The new season kicked off with a packed launch night featuring team introductions, uniform pickups, and a short skills showcase from our junior squads.

The evening gave new families a chance to meet coaches and committee members, while returning players got a preview of upcoming fixtures, training blocks, and community events.

We are especially grateful to our volunteers, who helped the night run smoothly from the registration desk to the canteen.
