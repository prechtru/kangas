## More than a team

Kangas Community Basketball Club is built around belonging. We want first-time players to feel welcome, developing athletes to feel challenged, and families to feel proud to be part of the club.

## What you can expect

- Experienced volunteer coaches and team managers
- Clear communication across fixtures, training, and events
- Seasonal updates shared through the club news page
- Opportunities to contribute on and off the court
